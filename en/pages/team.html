Founding Partner.

Founder · Managing Partner

Gianluca Leotta

Business lawyer and entrepreneur with consolidated experience in corporate law, M&A, private equity and venture capital.

Among the pioneers of Italy's new economy, he combines legal and entrepreneurial vision in structuring complex finance and tech transactions, with specific focus on data-driven models and AI applications in digital and industrial contexts. Over more than twenty years, he has led turnarounds, M&A deals, fund formations, build-up transactions and structured investments across NPL, real estate and technology, while advising startups across multiple industrial sectors, with an approach inspired by top U.S. business-law practice: strategic, execution-oriented and focused on value creation for investors, founders and management teams.
