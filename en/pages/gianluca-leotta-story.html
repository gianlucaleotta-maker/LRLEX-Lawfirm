Professional Story.

A path told through the press, from 2000 to today.

In the early 2000s, outlets such as Panorama, la Repubblica Affari & Finanza, Il Mondo, ItaliaOggi, Capital, Office Automation and Linea EDP followed a young entrepreneur working to consolidate independent internet operators in order to compete with the major telecommunications groups. That season ended with the market crash and the bursting of the internet bubble in 2001, which reshaped the entire sector.

From there, a different path. The Master of II Level in Private Equity, Politecnico di Milano (2004). Then the career as a business lawyer, articulated across two complementary areas. In investment funds and investment schemes, experience that reached the conception and structuring, in collaboration with ABI and the Italian banking system, of the "Fondo Valorizzazione Imprese" scheme, subsequently replicated by private equity operators. And in M&A and corporate restructuring transactions, build-ups, extraordinary operations and private equity practices, alongside legal contributions to specialised publications, until the move to projects managed directly.

And finally, the return to the arena, as an investor and protagonist of transactions, always between information technology and finance. In 2012, Milano Venture completed the acquisition of Audiencerate, a UK Audience Data Management company. Twelve years later, in 2024, Audiencerate joined the Microsoft partner ecosystem. In 2025 it obtained Google Customer Match Partner status. In 2026, the three-way partnership with Microsoft Italia and Postel (Poste Italiane Group) brought AI and data into service for Italian SMEs.

This is the thread that ties these articles together, the same idea, creating value by uniting what is fragmented, seen at different moments and from both sides of the table.
