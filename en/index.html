02 / The firm

A boutique built for business.

LR LEX advises across corporate and business law. The firm focuses on transactions and special situations, assisting companies, industrial groups, investment funds and financial institutions in Italy and internationally.

Our approach combines the technical quality of international firms with the responsiveness and partner-level attention that only a boutique can deliver. Every significant mandate is led by a Partner.

Based in Foro Buonaparte, Milan, we operate cross-border through a select network of correspondent firms in the United Kingdom and the United States.
